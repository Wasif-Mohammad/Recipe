Biryani Recipe

Biryani is a delicious and aromatic rice dish made with spices, rice, and usually meat or vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice - 2 cups
  • Chicken - 500g, cut into pieces
  • Onions - 2 large, thinly sliced
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, chopped
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 2 tbsp
  • Biryani masala - 2 tbsp
  • Saffron strands - a pinch, soaked in milk
  • Fried onions - 1/2 cup, for garnish
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  • Mint leaves - for garnish
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 3 tbsp
  • Ghee - 2 tbsp
  • Water - 3 cups

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil and ghee. Fry the thinly sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and biryani masala. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add chicken pieces, yogurt, salt, and cook until chicken is tender.
  5. In a separate pot, boil water and add soaked rice. Cook until rice is 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  6. In a large heavy-bottomed pot, layer the chicken masala and rice. Sprinkle fried onions, saffron milk, coriander, and mint leaves between the layers.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.
  8. Fluff gently with a fork and serve hot with raita and salad.
